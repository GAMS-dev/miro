Welcome!

Welcome to the documentation of GAMS MIRO (Model Interface for Rapid Orchestration) for and from GAMS, the General Algebraic Modeling System. GAMS MIRO is a graphical user interface (GUI) for your GAMS models that runs in the browser. With this interface you can supply your models with input data and generate, display and save the results of your optimization. Various graphics tools visually support your data to get a deep insight into the optimization problem.

GAMS MIRO is currently in the alpha phase. If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions for improvement, please contact support@gams.com.




Why should I use MIRO?

Are you working with GAMS and looking for a fast way to deploy your GAMS models automatically?
Do you have to make decisions based on optimization problems and are looking for an intuitive visualization tool?

Then GAMS MIRO is a good choice for you!

  • Quick & automated deployment of GAMS models
  • Data visualization with powerful charts / graphics
  • Generation, processing, evaluation & storing of scenario data
  • Creation & analysis of performance statistics
  • (Sensitivity) analysis of the scalar parameter range of a model
  • Intuitive use of a model without GAMS knowledge
  • Easy to use & comfortable working environment
  • Highly customizable

We have developed this tool to extend the possibilities of working with your GAMS models.
