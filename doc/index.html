Welcome!

Welcome to the documentation for the new WebUI for and from GAMS, the General Algebraic Modeling System. The GAMS WebUI is a graphical user interface (GUI) for your GAMS models that runs inside your browser. This interface allows you to feed your models with input data as well as generate and view the results of your optimization. Various graphing tools support your data visually to get the most insight out of your GAMS models.

GAMS WebUI is currently in the alpha phase. If you encounter any bugs or have suggestions for improvement, please contact support@gams.com.
